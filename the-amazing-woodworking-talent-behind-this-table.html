The amazing woodworking talent behind this table

October 14, 2025

About a year into opening our doors, Fred called us to ask if we would accept a table to be donated to the space so the coffee community could enjoy it. He had built this beautiful custom chess table & chairs and felt that Holm was a fitting place for it.

Ever since it was dropped off, it has felt right at home.
We have had the privilege to witness amazing humans connecting around it, for a cheeky game of chess, monopoly deal, brownies dates, laptop work, new friendships & lots of coffee drinking.

We're stoked to be showcasing it, and if you've had the honour to sit at the chess table, you know it's good!

This is a big thank you to Fred.

And to all of you, next time you're grabbing a coffee, take the time & slow down, ask for your coffee in a cute ceramic mug, and play a cheeky game. ♟️
